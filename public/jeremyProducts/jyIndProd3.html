Irae Serpentis Caelestis

"Wrath of the Celestial Serpent"

This dramatic illustration depicts a terrifying scene from legend or myth along the Great Wall of China. A colossal, dragon-like beast emerges from the turbulent storm clouds, its gaping maw and thrashing form radiating menace as lightning crackles around it. Along the iconic fortifications, tiny human figures stand dwarfed and powerless, their postures and gestures suggesting awe, fear and desperation in the face of this otherworldly, destructive force descending upon them. The juxtaposition of the grand, man-made architectural marvel against the overwhelming presence of the mythical winged serpent highlights the fragility of even humanity's greatest endeavors when confronted by the untamed powers and malevolent entities of ancient folklore and superstition.
